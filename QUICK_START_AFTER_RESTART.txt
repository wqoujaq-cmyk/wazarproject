QUICK START - Build APK After Restart
========================================

1. Restart your laptop

2. After restart, go to: D:\Wazar Project\

3. Right-click on: BUILD_APK.bat

4. Click "Run as administrator"

5. WAIT 15-20 minutes (don't close the window!)

6. When done, you'll see:
   "BUILD SUCCESSFUL!"
   
7. A folder will open with app-debug.apk

8. Copy app-debug.apk to your phone and install!
