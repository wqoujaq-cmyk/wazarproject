## Follow These Steps Exactly:

---

## **Step 1: Open Project in Android Studio**

1. **Open Android Studio** (you already have it open)
2. Click **"Open"** (the folder icon)
3. Navigate to: `D:\Wazar Project\UniversityEVotingApp\android`
4. Select the **android** folder
5. Click **"OK"**

---

## **Step 2: Wait for Gradle Sync**

- Android Studio will start "Gradle Build"
- You'll see a progress bar at the bottom
- **Wait 5-10 minutes** for it to finish
- It might download dependencies automatically
- Wait until you see "Gradle sync finished" or "Build completed"

---

## **Step 3: Build APK**

After Gradle sync finishes:

1. Click **"Build"** in the top menu
2. Click **"Build Bundle(s) / APK(s)"**
3. Click **"Build APK(s)"**
4. **Wait 10-15 minutes**
5. Android Studio will show a notification when done

---

## **Step 4: Locate Your APK**

When build succeeds, you'll see a notification at bottom right:

"APK(s) generated successfully"

Click **"locate"** in the notification, or find it manually at:

```
D:\Wazar Project\UniversityEVotingApp\android\app\build\outputs\apk\debug\app-debug.apk
```

---

## ‚úÖ **Then Install on Phone!**

Transfer the APK to your Android phone and install it!
