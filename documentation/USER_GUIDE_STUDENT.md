## 2. Registration

### Step-by-Step Registration

1. **Open the App**
   - Launch the E-Voting app
   - Tap "Register" on the login screen

2. **Fill in Your Information**
   - **Full Name:** Enter your complete name
   - **University ID:** Enter your student ID (e.g., ENG001, MED123)
   - **Faculty:** Select your faculty from the dropdown
   - **Major:** Enter your major/department
   - **Password:** Create a strong password (min. 8 characters)
   - **Confirm Password:** Re-enter your password
   - **Profile Picture (Optional):** Tap the camera icon to add a photo

3. **Submit Registration**
   - Review your information
   - Tap "Register"
   - Wait for confirmation

4. **Success!**
   - You'll see a success message
   - You can now login with your credentials

### Important Notes

- Use your **official university ID**
- Remember your password - it cannot be recovered easily
- Your faculty determines which elections you can vote in

### Logging In

   - You'll be taken to the main screen

## 4. Managing Your Profile

### Viewing Your Profile

1. Tap the **"Profile"** tab at the bottom
2. You'll see:
   - Your profile picture
   - Name
   - University ID
   - Faculty and Major
   - Account status

### Updating Your Profile Picture

1. Go to Profile tab
2. Tap on your profile picture
3. Choose "Change Photo"
4. Select a photo from your device or take a new one
5. Tap "Save"

### Updating Your Major

1. Go to Profile tab
2. Tap "Edit Profile"
3. Update your major
4. Tap "Save"

**Note:** You cannot change your University ID or Faculty. Contact an administrator if these need to be corrected.

### Logging Out

1. Go to Profile tab
2. Scroll down
3. Tap "Logout"
4. Confirm logout

## 7. Viewing Results

### Election Results

1. Go to **Elections** tab
2. Find a **closed** election
3. Tap on the election
4. Tap "View Results"
5. You'll see:
   - Winner and vote counts
   - All candidates ranked
   - Vote percentages
   - Vote distribution chart

### Poll Results

1. Go to **Polls** tab
2. Find a **closed** poll
3. Tap on the poll
4. Tap "View Results"
5. You'll see:
   - Most popular answer
   - All responses with counts
   - Percentage distribution
   - Visual chart

**Note:** Results are only available after elections/polls close.

## 8. Troubleshooting

### Cannot Login

**Problem:** "Invalid credentials" error

**Solution:**
- Double-check your University ID
- Ensure password is correct (case-sensitive)
- Make sure your account is active
- Contact administrator if problem persists

### Cannot See Elections

**Problem:** No elections showing

**Possible Reasons:**
- No active elections for your faculty
- Pull down to refresh the list
- Check your internet connection
- Elections may be scheduled for future dates

### Cannot Vote

**Problem:** "You have already voted" error

**Reason:** You can only vote once per election

**Problem:** "Election not active" error

**Reason:** Election has closed or hasn't started yet

### App is Slow

**Solutions:**
- Check your internet connection
- Close and restart the app
- Clear app cache (in device settings)
- Update to latest version

### Forgot Password

**Solution:**
- Contact your system administrator
- Provide your University ID for verification
- Administrator will reset your password

### Photo Upload Failed

**Solutions:**
- Check file size (must be under 5MB)
- Use JPG or PNG format
- Ensure app has camera/gallery permissions
- Try a different photo

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is my vote anonymous?

Yes! Your vote is confidential. Results show only vote counts, not individual votes.

### Can I change my vote?

No. Once you confirm your vote, it is final and cannot be changed.

### Can I vote multiple times?

No. The system allows only one vote per user per election/poll.

### What if I make a mistake selecting a candidate?

Use the "Go Back" button before confirming. Once you tap "Confirm & Submit Vote," it's final.

### Can I vote from multiple devices?

Yes, but you still get only one vote. The system tracks votes by your account, not your device.

### What happens if my app crashes during voting?

If the vote wasn't confirmed, you can try again. If it was confirmed, your vote is saved.

### When can I see results?

Results are available after the election or poll closes.

### Can I vote offline?

No. You need an internet connection to participate in elections and polls.
